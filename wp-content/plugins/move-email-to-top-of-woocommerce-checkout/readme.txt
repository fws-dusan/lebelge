Moves email to the top of the checkout field to capture email early.

== Description ==

Moves email to the first field of the checkout fields. Encourages customers to enter their email early in the checkout field, which should be used in conjunction with an abandon cart or email capture plugin.

Features
*   **Lightweight** - does not slow down your admin dashboard or frontend
*   **Mobile-friendly** - compatible with mobile devices

== Installation ==

1. Upload `move-email-to-top.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
